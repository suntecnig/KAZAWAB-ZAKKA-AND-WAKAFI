Our Mission

The Katsina State Zakat and Waqf Board (KAZAWAB), launched in 2023, transforms Islamic philanthropy with cutting-edge technology. Our blockchain-powered platform ensures transparent, Shariah-compliant Zakat and Waqf management, uplifting communities worldwide.

Mission: To empower communities through innovative, transparent Zakat and Waqf solutions.

Vision: A global community united by compassion, driven by technology, and thriving in prosperity.

About Zakat

Zakat Essentials

Join millions in fulfilling Zakat, a sacred pillar of Islam. By giving 2.5% of your qualifying wealth annually, you support the poor, needy, and others, purifying your wealth and earning divine blessings.

Zakat Benefits

Embrace Zakat to grow spiritually and uplift society. Your contributions reduce inequality, empower communities, and bring you closer to Allah. Our platform ensures instant, verified impact.

About Waqf

Waqf Fundamentals

Create a lasting legacy with Waqf, a perpetual endowment. Dedicate assets like cash or property to fund schools, hospitals, or mosques, earning continuous rewards (Sadaqah Jariyah) for generations.

Waqf Impact

Your Waqf transforms lives by sustaining education, healthcare, and more. With our blockchain registry, your endowment is managed transparently, ensuring eternal rewards and community growth.

No, Waqf is typically permanent and irrevocable, but temporary cash Waqf is permissible in some cases.
